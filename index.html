About

Hi, I’m Prashant Jha

Buying or selling a home is one of the biggest decisions someone has to make in their life. Finding the right professional is essential to help you navigate today’s fast paced market who can make your Real Estate experience smooth and memorable.

While growing up, I have always had passion about Technology, Traveling and Creativity. After graduating from Ryerson University I spent my career in Technology & Project Management working in various Govt. & private sectors. I have put on many hats since young age. With my love & passion for Real Estate, I decided to pursue my career in Real Estate. With my diverse background and experience, I understand the importance of focusing on detail, understanding client needs & deliver the results.

People who know me will tell you that I have great people’s skills, I’m persistent, I pay attention to the details and I like to WIN !

So, If you or someone you know is planning to make a real estate move, now is the perfect time! Sit back, relax and let me take care of your Real Estate needs!
